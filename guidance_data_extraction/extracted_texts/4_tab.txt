[[[{ENC}CleverTap SDK encrypts all data in transit]]]..  No - Answer  No  if your app or a third party does not encrypt the collected user data.

[[[{DEL}CleverTap provides a way to request the deletion of data. Refer to  Delete User API]]].  No - Answer  No  if you do not provide your users with an option to delete their data.
